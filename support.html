SUPPORT AIMED TODAY

Every year AIMED raises money from thousands of supporters to support programs and services for individuals with intellectual disabilities and/or developmental delays and their families.

Donations are used to pay for programs and services to support our participants when they or families don’t have resources. We also use these funds to create or expand new innovative programs that benefit our participants.

AIMED has a solid reputation for being a good steward of gifts we receive. We have been noted as being a well-managed nonprofit organization in the industry.

A gift to AIMED Human Services may entitle you to a charitable contribution deduction against your income tax.

ABOUT US

We believe in a personalized therapeutic approach, addressing the unique needs of everyone in our community. Our goal is to create a safe, caring, and homelike environment. We achieve this by taking the time to understand each participant’s preferences, ensuring our space truly feels like “home.”
